Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3rd April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Book Corners collects, uses and protects personal data in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Italian data protection laws.

1. Data Controller

The Data Controller is the operator of Book Corners, based in Italy.

For any privacy related request, you can contact:

Andrea Grandi
Email: mail@andreagrandi.it

2. What personal data we collect

We collect only the personal data necessary to provide and operate the service.

Account data

If you create an account, we may collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely in hashed form)

If you sign in using Google, we receive basic account information from Google, such as your email address and a unique identifier necessary to authenticate your account.

User generated content

When you submit a book corner or report an issue, we process the information you voluntarily provide, which may include:

  • Location name and description
  • Address or map coordinates
  • Photos
  • Reports or comments

Technical data

To operate and secure the website, we may process limited technical data such as:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Browser and device information

3. Purposes and legal basis of processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

Providing the service

To create and manage user accounts, publish and moderate submissions, and operate the map and directory.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Security and abuse prevention

To protect the website, prevent fraud, spam or misuse.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Responding to communications

To reply to messages or support requests.

Legal basis: performance of a contract or legitimate interest.

We do not reuse personal data for purposes unrelated to providing Book Corners.

4. Data selling and marketing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share personal data for advertising purposes.

We do not plan to sell personal data in the future.

5. Data hosting and transfers

Book Corners is hosted on a virtual private server provided by Hetzner, with infrastructure located within the European Union.

Personal data is stored and processed within the EU. No transfers outside the European Economic Area are carried out unless required by third party authentication providers such as Google, in which case such providers act according to their own privacy policies and applicable safeguards.

6. Data retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service.

  • Account data is kept while the account is active.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Technical logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes.

Public submissions may remain visible unless removal is requested.

7. Recipients of data

Personal data may be processed by service providers strictly necessary for operating the website, such as hosting providers. These providers act as data processors under Article 28 GDPR.

Personal data may also be disclosed where required by law or by a competent authority.

8. Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Request data portability

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali).

To exercise your rights, contact: mail@andreagrandi.it

9. Security measures

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies

Book Corners uses a small number of cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. No tracking, analytics or advertising cookies are used.

Cookies we set

  • Session cookie (sessionid) — keeps you logged in while you use the site. Expires after 2 weeks or when you log out.
  • CSRF cookie (csrftoken) — protects form submissions against cross-site request forgery. Expires after 1 year.
  • Language cookie (django_language) — remembers your preferred language. Expires at the end of the browser session.

Why no consent banner is shown

Under the ePrivacy Directive (Article 5(3)) and Italian data protection guidelines, cookies that are strictly necessary for providing the service do not require user consent. All cookies listed above fall within this exemption.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
